Lesson 1: What Is a Computer?

Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will understand what a computer is and why it is important.

What Is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic machine that accepts data, processes it, stores it, and produces information.

What Can a Computer Do?

  • Type documents
  • Calculate numbers
  • Store information
  • Connect to the internet

Examples of Computers

Desktop computers, laptops, tablets, smartphones, and ATM machines.

Why Computers Are Important

Computers help people learn, work, communicate, and solve problems faster.

Summary

A computer is a powerful tool that helps us perform many tasks easily and quickly.
