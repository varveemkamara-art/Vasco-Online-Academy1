Lesson 6: Internet Basics

What Is the Internet?

The internet is a global system that connects millions of computers and devices around the world. It allows people to send information, share files, and communicate instantly, no matter where they are.

Through the internet, users can access websites, watch videos, read news, and use online services. It works by sending data through cables, satellites, and wireless networks.

The internet has become an important part of daily life for education, business, and entertainment. Students, teachers, and professionals all rely on it to get information quickly.

Uses of the Internet

The internet is used for many positive and useful activities that help people grow and stay connected. Some of the most common uses include the following:

  • Learning: Online classes, tutorials, and educational videos
  • Communication: Email, social media, and video calls
  • Research: Finding information for school, work, and projects
