Lesson 8: Online Safety

Why Safety Matters

Online safety is important because the internet has risks such as scams, hackers, and fake websites. If users are not careful, their personal information can be stolen or misused.

People use the internet for email, banking, school, and social media. Protecting accounts and personal details helps prevent fraud and identity theft.

By following basic safety rules, users can enjoy the internet while staying protected. Good online habits help reduce the chances of problems.

Safety Rules

The following safety rules help users stay safe while using the internet:

  • Do not share passwords: Keep passwords private and hard to guess
  • Avoid suspicious links: Do not click unknown links or pop-up messages
  • Log out on public computers: Always sign out after using cyber cafés or shared devices
