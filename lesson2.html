Lesson 2: Parts of a Computer

Lesson Objective

Learn the main physical parts of a computer and their functions.

What Is Hardware?

Hardware refers to the physical parts of a computer that you can see and touch.

Main Parts

  • Monitor: Displays information
  • CPU: The brain of the computer
  • Keyboard: Used for typing
  • Mouse: Used for clicking and selecting

Summary

Computer hardware works together to make the system function.
