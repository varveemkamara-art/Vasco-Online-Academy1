Lesson 7: Email Basics

What Is Email?

Email, short for electronic mail, is a way of sending messages over the internet. It allows users to send and receive messages instantly using computers or mobile phones.

An email message can include text, pictures, documents, and other files as attachments. To use email, a person needs an email address, such as name@example.com.

Emails are sent through email services like Gmail, Yahoo Mail, and Outlook. Once sent, the message reaches the receiver within seconds or minutes.

Why Email Is Important

Email is important because it helps people communicate quickly and professionally. It is commonly used in schools, offices, and businesses.

Students use email to receive assignments and communicate with teachers. Workers use email to send reports, applications, and official messages.

Email also helps users keep records of conversations and important information. Messages can be saved, searched, and accessed anytime from anywhere with internet access.
